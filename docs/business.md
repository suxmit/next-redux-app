Company Overview
LUXAGRO TAUREA LLP is an India-based agricultural company with long-standing experience in farming and domestic distribution. Built on a strong agricultural legacy, the company is now expanding into international markets, offering premium agricultural and allied products worldwide.
Our Background
With years of direct involvement in cultivation and supply, we have consistently served the Indian market with reliability. Our deep understanding of farming practices and seasonal cycles enables us to maintain quality from seed selection to harvest.

Our Brand Story
LUXAGRO TAUREA LLP was founded on a deep-rooted agricultural heritage shaped by years of hands-on farming and trusted domestic distribution across India. Our journey began in the fields, where knowledge of the land, respect for nature, and commitment to quality were cultivated over generations.
With extensive experience in cultivation, sourcing, and supply, we have proudly served the Indian market with consistency, integrity, and reliability. This strong domestic foundation reflects our belief that true quality is built from the ground up, starting with the right farming practices and lasting partnerships with farmers and suppliers.
Today, LUXAGRO TAUREA LLP is expanding beyond borders, bringing the richness of Indian agriculture to global markets. By combining traditional agricultural wisdom with modern processing, packaging, and export logistics, we deliver premium-quality products that meet international standards.
Our brand represents strength, trust, and refinement. As we expand internationally, we remain committed to fostering long-term relationships, maintaining transparent trade, and delivering excellence from trusted farms to global tables.
